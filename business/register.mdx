## Business Registration

Register new businesses in Nyra Wallet to start managing your business operations and create API clients for integrations.

## Prerequisites

<Info>
  You must be authenticated with a valid JWT token to register businesses.
</Info>

- Valid JWT token in `Authorization: Bearer <token>` header
- User account must be active and verified

## Registration Process

<Steps>
<Step title="Authenticate">
  Ensure you have a valid JWT token from the login endpoint.
  
  <Check>
  Token should be included in Authorization header.
  </Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Prepare Business Data">
  Gather the required business information:
  
  - Business name
  - Business address
  - Business type (from predefined categories)
  
  <Warning>
  Business type must match one of the predefined BusinessType enum values.
  </Warning>
</Step>

<Step title="Submit Registration">
  Send POST request to `/business/register` with business data.
  
  <Check>
  Business will be created and associated with your user account.
  </Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Create API Clients">
  After registration, create API clients for third-party integrations.
  
  <Check>
  API clients enable secure access to business data.
  </Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Required Information

### Business Details
- **Business Name**: The official name of your business
- **Address**: Physical or registered business address
- **Business Type**: Must match predefined BusinessType enum values

<Note>
  Business type values are predefined and cannot be customized. Contact support if you need a new business type added.
</Note>

## Business Types

The system supports predefined business types including:
- Retail
- Service
- Manufacturing
- Technology
- Healthcare
- Financial Services
- And more...

## Response

Upon successful registration, you'll receive:
- **Business ID**: Unique identifier for your business
- **Business Details**: Complete business information
- **Creator Information**: Your user ID as the business creator
- **Success Message**: Confirmation of successful registration
